How it works

Buy and sell Trending Topics

Tweetstockr is a stock exchange free-to-play game that allows the buying and selling of fantasy shares of the Twitter Trending Topics. Buy in low popularity, sell when everybory is tweeting about it.

Are you into the trends? Compete with your friends on daily and weekly tournaments. It's way more fun!

Get real rewards playing!

Tweetstockr is integrated with Joysticket - The loyalty program for games - so you can get Tickets playing on tournaments with your friends and redeem them for real rewards.

From PayPal money to Google Play Credits, there is a lot of prizes for you to choose!
